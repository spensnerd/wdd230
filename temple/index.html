Oklahoma City Temple

The Oklahom City Oklahoma temple was dedicated in 2000 and rededicated in 2019 by President Henry B. Eyring. It is located just north of Oklahoma city on the Northwest Expressway, just a short shuttle ride from one of our premier hotels. Its layout is slightly different than most small temples from the time as the baptismal font is to the left of the front desk and the other rooms are to the right.
