Long term missionary accomodations

If you are a temple missionary and would like to reserve a space for a week or longercontact us and we will happily discuss our rates and plans. We offer leases for those who wish to stay here and even have a few apartment style spaces for those looking to buy their own place, though rooms are limited in that regard.

Extended stay with kitchenette

If you find yourself in the position of wanting a small kitchen to cook a few meals while you're working at the temple we can accomodate you! Our extended stay suites are available for a week or more at a time and offer you a place to kick your shoes off. We even have a family history center here onsite so that you can look up names to bring to the temple.

Overnight visit only

You may also be in the position of just needing an overnight room as you are called to work on a single day. Rest assured we have a room for you! Our check in times are later than most hotels due to the nature of our business but our check out times are later as well. We take check in at 5 PM, and check out at 3 PM, so you can travel down and leave your things with us while working at the temple.
